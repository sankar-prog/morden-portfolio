Hi, I’m Sankar — a fresher web developer who speaks Python more fluently than my alarm clock at 6 a.m. I have a soft spot for Django, clean code, and making sure my database doesn’t run away screaming (thanks, MySQL).



I love taking random ideas, giving them coffee, and turning them into fully functional projects. I’ve built a few personal projects already, and each one taught me two things:



1. Git is my friend (most days).




2. Debugging at 2 a.m. counts as cardio.





While I’m still early in my career, I’m eager to learn from experienced developers, absorb wisdom like a sponge (a clean one, not the kitchen sink kind), and grow into a developer who writes code today that I can still understand tomorrow.


 
I also believe good communication and teamwork are just as important as good code — which is why I actually enjoy group discussions, collaborating, and receiving feedback (as long as it comes with snacks).



When I’m not coding, you’ll find me smashing volleyballs, pretending to be a chess grandmaster, traveling, binge-watching movies, or daydreaming about my next project. I’m based in Chennai, proudly from Dharmapuri, and always ready for challenges with a positive (and slightly caffeinated) mindset.
